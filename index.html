CHENNAI TOURIST SPOTS

Marina beach

Marina Beach,is a natural urban beach in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.It is the second longest urban beach in the world.

St George Fort

Fort St. George is a fortress at the coastal city of Chennai, India. Founded in 1639, it was the first English fortress in India.

Kapaleeshwarar temple

Kapaleeshwarar Temple is dedicated to lord Shiva.Shiva's consort Parvati worshipped at this temple is called Karpagambal.The temple was built around the 7th century CE in Dravidian architecture.

Santhome Church

San Thome Church, officially known as St Thomas Cathedral Basilica and National Shrine of Saint Thomas, is a minor basilica of the Catholic Church in India.

Elliots beach

Edward Elliot's Beach, simply called as Elliot's Beach and popularly known as Besant Nagar Beach or the Bessie, is a natural urban beach located in the Besant Nagar

Govt Museum

The Government Museum,or the Madras Museum,is a museum of human history and culture located in the Government Museum Complex in Egmore in Chennai, India. Started in 1851, it is the second oldest museum in India.

Guindy park

War memorial

Victory War Memorial,is a memorial in Chennai, India,Became the victory war memorial for World War II,erected in the memory of those from the Madras presidency who died in the wars.

Vadapalani Murugan temple

Vadapalani Andavar Temple is dedicated to Lord Muruga. It is located in Vadapalani,Chennai.It was renovated in the 1920s and a Rajagopuram was built.The temple is popular,due to the patronage of cinema stars.

Tower Park

Anna Nagar Tower Park, officially known as Dr Visvesvaraya Tower Park, is an urban park in the suburb of Anna Nagar, Chennai. It is the tallest park tower in Chennai
